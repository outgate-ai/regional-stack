DETECTION CRITERIA: Confidential Business Information

Detect confidential business information:
- Trade secrets and proprietary data
- Internal financial reports (marked CONFIDENTIAL, INTERNAL)
- Unreleased product information
- Strategic business plans
- Employee/customer databases
- Competitive intelligence marked as confidential

You should **NOT** detect relative or absolute file system paths such as /home/tux/example.txt or ~/tux/example.txt
